Principal's Desk

I deem it a great privilege and honour to present here a brief history of Nagendra Jha Mahila College, Laheriasarai. Situated in the heartland of Mithila region, this college is a dream destination for girl students of this locality. This college is named after Dr. Nagendra Jha, Who was a self – made man. He had a humble beginning as a lecturer at C.M. College and by dint of his amiable nature and hard work rose to become education minister of Bihar. It was his abiding love for his place and Mithila that prompted his well-wishers to open this University Darbhanga. I will take this opportunity to express my gratitude towards university authorities for their kind co-operation and proper guidance.
I feel pride in announcing that teachers of this college are involved in other academic activities for enrichment of their academic career such as research work and publication, organizing seminars and attending orientation course and refresher course. This college plans to make available all these opportunities to other teacher also. Multiple welfare schemes will be started shortly for non-teaching staffs by the college management. This college publishes college magazine "Jyoti" annually and plans to get it registered it near future.
